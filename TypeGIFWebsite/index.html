What is TypeGIF?


TypeGIF is an iOS app with a custom keyboard that allows users to search for GIFs using the Giphy API, and add them to their own personal collections. Collections of GIFs that users have saved are displayed in the TypeGIF keyboard in Messages and other messaging apps, giving users instant access to all their favorite GIFs.

Check out GIFs in one of our featured categories. Save GIFs to a collection by double-tapping them, or copy them to your phone's clipboard with a long press.

Search for a specific term, or scroll through recently trending GIFs. Select one of your own custom collections to save GIFs for later.

Manage your personal GIF collections
in the Favorites tab. Add new collections or delete the ones that you've outgrown.

Check out your saved GIFs by selecting
a collection. Copy GIFs to the clipboard
by pressing once, or delete GIFs.

Use your saved GIFs in your own customized
GIF keyboard while composing messages in Apple's Messages and Mail apps.
